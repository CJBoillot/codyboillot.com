What I Build

Platforms that solve real problems

A selection of internal platforms I conceived, built, and launched, each adopted into daily use to remove friction and unlock the team.

AI app-generation platform

An internal AI-powered platform that lets engineers and non-engineers spin up working applications from natural-language prompts, bringing rapid prototyping in-house and removing dependence on external low-code vendors.

Self-service deployment platform

A self-service system that lets users stand up applications on AWS behind company SSO with a custom URL, turning a manual, engineering-gated process into a few-click workflow and dramatically shortening time-to-launch.

Gamified engineer training platform

A gamified learning platform that helps engineers target and close individual skill gaps using badges and achievements, central to the program that graduated 60+ junior engineers into independent contributors.

Broadcast operations portal

A TED-style talk series where volunteers present on a topic of their choice, building a culture of education and knowledge-sharing while developing presentation and public-speaking skills across the org.
